SUNRISE

Sunrise is influenced by various factors such as geographic location, time of year, and atmospheric conditions. It's often considered a visually stunning and symbolic event, inspiring artists, poets, and photographers throughout history.

SUNSET

Sunset refers to the moment when the upper edge of the Sun disappears below the horizon in the evening, marking the end of daylight and the beginning of twilight. Like sunrise, sunset is influenced by factors such as geographic location, time of year, and atmospheric conditions.

MOON

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite, orbiting around our planet approximately once every 27.3 days. It plays a significant role in various aspects of Earth, including tides, calendars, and cultural symbolism
